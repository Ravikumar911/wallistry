OUR COLLECTION

Each notebook has a story and is uniquely designed, drawing inspiration from the aesthetics of India.

NAMAT

Inspired by the rich geometrical patterns of the Paigah complex, “Namat”, displays the beauty of the Mughal art.

HIMALAYAN MUSK

The five petal floral pattern is inspired from the elegant Himalayan musk flowers, found in the Himalayas.

KANCHI

The mango art is inspired from the patterns found in the traditional hand-woven Kanchipuram silk sarees.
