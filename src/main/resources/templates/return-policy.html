RETURN POLICY

We at Wallistry, take utmost care from design to until the product is shipped to you. But if you are unhappy with our products or if you receive it in damaged condition you can choose to return to us as follows.


What is our return policy?


At Wallistry we process only store credit/replacements and no direct refunds. You will either receive a store credit or replacement for the following conditions.

1. If you are unhappy with our product and wish to return or exchange an item, please email our Customer Care at wallistry@gmail.com.

The item(s) should be unused, unsoiled and unwashed.

The packaging should have the original packaging and tags in place.

Any returned item received by us that does not meet the above mentioned conditions will not be accepted. No amount will be reimbursed in this case.

For the above condition, you will get a Wallistry store credit which can be used for your future purchase with us.

2. If you receive the product in a damaged condition, you can email us with pictures or call us within 48 hours of delivery. Our team will assess the damage and will get back to you immediately.

For the above condition, you will receive a replacement of the same product of the same product value.
