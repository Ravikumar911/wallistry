FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When will I get my order delivered?

Since most of our products are handmade, we usually take a time of 3-10 days to get the order reach you.

How much do you charge for shipping?

It is absolutely free for purchase above Rs. 600. We charge a small amount of Rs.50 for orders below it (domestic orders).

Do you ship internationally?

We usually don’t but we can make some exceptions for you. Do mail us at wallistry@gmail.com and we will discuss in detail.

How do I place bulk orders?

We know you love our products and if you do want to order more of it, do visit our Corporate page or mail us at wallistry@gmail.com.

What if I wish to return the product?

Oh no! We are very sure you will love us and our products and if you still want to return, we do offer you a store credit. We don’t have a pick up option just yet but do contact us or visit the Returns policy page for more details

Note:

We do not refund in cash.

What do I do if I receive a damaged item?.

Hope you don’t but if you do, you can return to us and we will replace it to you with a new piece. We don’t have a pick up option just yet but do contact us and check out the Returns policy page for more details.
