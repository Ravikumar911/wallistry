BHUMI

TERRACOTTA WATER BOTTLE

The terracotta water bottles are crafted by the fine artisans of Tamil Nadu. “Bhumi” means “Earth” in the Tamil language. Made from clay, “Bhumi” is crafted to inspire a healthier lifestyle, reviving the traditional use of earthenware, designed with a contemporary outlook.
